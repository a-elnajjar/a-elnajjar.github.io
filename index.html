About CardsKidsGame

CardsKidsGame is an engaging and educational app designed for children. The app uses emoji flash cards and voice play to create a fun learning environment. Our primary focus is on delivering an enjoyable and safe experience for all users.

Privacy Policy

Information Collection and Use

  • No Information Collection: CardsKidsGame does not collect any personal information from its users. We respect your privacy and ensure that your data remains completely private.
  • No Login Required: CardsKidsGame does not require any login or account creation. You can enjoy all the features of our app without needing to provide any personal details or sign up for an account.

Data Security

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no risk of your data being accessed, used, or disclosed. Your privacy and security are our top priorities.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in this README file, and we encourage you to review it periodically.

Usage Instructions

  1. Install the App: Download and install CardsKidsGame from the app store.
  2. Launch the App: Open the app on your device.
  3. Start Playing: Select a game mode and begin playing with the emoji flash cards and voice play features.
