Privacy Policy

App Instructions

CardsKidsGame

  1. Install from the App Store.
  2. Select a category (alphabet, numbers, shapes, fruits).
  3. Tap cards to hear voice feedback and explore learning interactively.

Strike Lite – Soccer Coach

  1. Download from the App Store.
  2. Use the field canvas to draw tactics using lines and icons.
  3. Save and share game plans with your team or staff.

Hoop Basketball Whiteboard

  1. Install from the App Store.
  2. Drag and place players on the court.
  3. Draw plays and export strategies for practice or game day.

Cards Kids Peek Game

  1. Tap the screen to reveal hidden fruit cards.
  2. Learn fruit names with playful sound feedback.
